# Analytos Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) Guide

## Segments

### Segment 1: Mid-Market Retail
- **Industry**: Retail and Logistics
- **Company Size**: Mid-sized ($50M - $500M)
- **Primary Product Fit**: Stockly
- **Target Personas**: Supply Chain Manager, Operations Director

### Segment 2: Enterprise Healthcare
- **Industry**: Healthcare, Medical Device Manufacturing, Pharmaceuticals
- **Company Size**: Enterprise ($500M+)
- **Primary Product Fit**: Inspectly
- **Target Personas**: Chief Compliance Officer (CCO), QA Director

## Persona Pain Points

- **Supply Chain Manager**: Exhausted by manual excel sheets to track inventory. High risk of stockouts during peak seasons.
- **Operations Director**: Lacks a unified dashboard to see where money is tied up in slow-moving stock.
- **Chief Compliance Officer (CCO)**: Terrified of FDA or SOC2 audit failures. Needs bulletproof documentation.
- **QA Director**: Manual sampling and log checking takes too many man-hours.
