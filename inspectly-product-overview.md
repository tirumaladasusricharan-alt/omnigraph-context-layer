# Inspectly Product Overview

## Introduction
Inspectly is Analytos's premier compliance and quality assurance platform. It provides automated monitoring and reporting for strict regulatory environments.

## Key Features
- **Auto-Audit Log**: Captures every system change with cryptographic signing to ensure tampering is impossible.
- **Compliance Scanner**: Runs daily checks against HIPAA, SOC2, and ISO27001 control frameworks.

## Proof Points & Metrics
- **Audit Prep Time**: Reduced average compliance audit preparation time by 60% (from 4 weeks to 1.5 weeks).
- **Defect Rate**: Customers report a 25% drop in regulatory defects within the first 6 months of use.

## Target Market
Inspectly targets the Healthcare and Medical Device Manufacturing industries, prioritizing enterprise companies ($500M+ annual revenue).

## Target Personas
- **Chief Compliance Officer (CCO)**: Overwhelmed by changing regulations; needs guaranteed audit trails.
- **QA Director**: Spends too much time manually checking logs; wants automated defect reporting.
