# Stockly Product Overview

## Introduction
Stockly is an advanced supply chain management product designed by Analytos to modernize inventory control. It provides end-to-end visibility into stock levels, forecasting, and automated replenishment.

## Key Features
- **Auto-Restock**: Automatically generates purchase orders when inventory levels fall below a dynamic threshold.
- **Demand Forecasting Engine**: Uses historical sales data and seasonal trends to predict future inventory needs.

## Proof Points & Metrics
- **Conversion Rate**: Improved by 15% over a 3-month period by ensuring high-demand items are never out of stock.
- **Processing Speed**: Reduced order processing time by 40%, from 2.5 hours to 1.5 hours per batch.

## Target Market
Stockly primarily targets the Retail and Logistics industries, specifically focusing on mid-sized companies ($50M - $500M annual revenue).

## Target Personas
- **Supply Chain Manager**: Struggles with manual stockouts and overstocking issues. Wants automated control.
- **Operations Director**: Needs high-level visibility and reporting on supply chain efficiency.
